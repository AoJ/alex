Instructions

To participate in this hit, dial a USA toll free phone number displayed above the feedback form and you will be connect with the automated tourist information service. If you call from a USA land line, than the calls should be completely free.

Please talk naturally. You will be given a task which will require you to ask for information about bars, restaurants, hotels, colleges, etc. The service can pick up what you are saying, ask you questions and can correct its own mistakes while the dialogue goes along.

  • If you are asked about something which is not specified in the task you should say that you do not care, or that that information does not matter. Please do NOT make something up that is not in the description.
  • If during the call it turns out there is nothing matching the task description, you should try to find the closest alternative (e.g. if there is no cheap bar then you might want to look for bars in the moderate pricerange).
  • In order to finish just say "Thank you. Good bye." to the system.

Once the call ended, you will be asked 4 questions about your experiences:

  • Q1. Did you find all the information you were looking for? [ Yes / No ]
  • Q2. The system understood me well.
    [ strongly disagree / disagree / slightly disagree / slightly agree / agree / strongly agree ]
  • Q3. The phrasing of the system's responses was good.
    [ strongly disagree / disagree / slightly disagree / slightly agree / agree / strongly agree ]
  • Q4. The system's voice was of good quality.
    [ strongly disagree / disagree / slightly disagree / slightly agree / agree / strongly agree ]

At the end of the call, the system will give you a four digit code which you have to enter into the feedback form. This is used to verify the genuine HIT submissions. Once the call is finished, please fill in the feedback form. Please enter the provided code, answer the questions and submit the results.

Sending the feedback is very important as only the calls with completed feedback and the correct code will be approved.

Note that for research purposes all calls are recorded. Please read this consent form carefully before starting. By participating in this HIT, you agree with the terms and conditions stated in the consent form.
