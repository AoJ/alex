STATEMENT OF CONSENT

You have volunteered to participate in a study conducted in the Institute of Formal and Applied Linguistics at the Charles University in Prague.

This study is concerned with how people interact with spoken dialogue systems. In the course of this study, you will be interacting with different dialogue systems. All interactions with the dialogue system will be recorded for further study. After completion of the task(s) you will be asked several questions about your experiences. There are no known expected physical discomforts or risks involved in your participation.

For the purposes of the Czech Republic 101/2000 Data Protection Act, you give your permission for audio and video recordings, transcriptions, and non-identifying information (e.g. gender, age, mother tongue) to be electronically stored, utilised and shared for purposes of research, development, and commercial deployment of spoken dialogue systems and similar applications involving spoken speech. No identifying information, gathered in this study will be disclosed to any persons other than the researchers and their collaborators.

Results of this study will appear in academic journals in terms of group data. If any individual data will be presented, the data will be totally anonymous, without any means of identifying the individuals involved. The researchers will be glad to answer any questions about this study. However, answers that could influence the outcome of the study will be deferred until you have completed the study.

Every effort is made to conduct this study in compliance with the appropriate professional guidelines.
